ABOUT

Where We Started
EasyPC is a computer retail business with branches found throughout NCR.
We focus on being convinient to customers, on top of providing quality customer service.
EasyPC was founded in 2009 as a secondhand PC reseller and quickly found its way to be one of the leading computer retailers in the country.

Where We're Going
We have grown throughout the years, while still offering you a wide range of products.
Regardless of this, offering the best customer service is still our goal.

Our Mission

powered by our technical expertise, passion for innovation, and customer-centric philosophy."

Our Vision
"EasyPC is committed to being the most trusted partner of Filipino households and growing businesses for
computer essentials by providing exceptional customer experience built on ease, technical expertise, and empathy."
